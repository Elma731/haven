About Haven

Nigeria's Most Trusted Property Platform

Founded in 2020, Haven was built with a simple mission: to make finding your perfect home in Nigeria easier, faster and more trustworthy. We verify every listing, vet every agent, and provide the tools you need to make confident property decisions.
