Luxury Beachfront Villa

Banana Island, Lagos, Nigeria
FOR SALE LUXURY NEW LISTING
🛏 6 Beds 🚿 5 Baths 📐 680 sqm 🚗 4 Parking
₦850,000,000

This extraordinary beachfront villa represents the pinnacle of luxury living in Nigeria. Situated on the exclusive Banana Island, it offers unobstructed views of the Atlantic Ocean from every floor. Designed by award-winning architect Bayo Akinyemi, the property seamlessly blends contemporary Nigerian aesthetics with world-class finishes.

Spanning three floors over 680 square metres of living space, this magnificent residence features six en-suite bedrooms, a grand open-plan living area with double-height ceilings, and a chef's kitchen equipped with premium German appliances. The master suite occupies the entire top floor, with a private terrace overlooking the ocean.

The landscaped grounds include a 20-metre infinity pool, a fully equipped private gym, home cinema, and direct gated beach access. The property is supported by 100% solar power backup, fibre internet throughout, and a comprehensive Lutron smart home system. Banana Island's 24/7 security and controlled access ensure complete peace of mind.

Key Highlights

  • Direct beach access through private gated walkway
  • 20-metre infinity swimming pool with ocean views
  • Lutron smart home automation throughout
  • 24/7 security with CCTV and BQ accommodation
  • 100% solar power backup with inverter system
  • Fully fitted German kitchen with premium appliances
  • Home cinema room with 4K projection
  • 4-car garage with EV charging points
